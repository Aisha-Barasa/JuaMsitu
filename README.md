## JUAMSITU – Know Your Forest. Protect Your Future

JuaMsitu is an **intelligent environmental platform** that transforms ecological data into clear, actionable insights. It helps communities, conservationists, and researchers **understand forest health at a glance** through dynamic scoring, trend analysis, and AI-powered predictions.

---

## Key Features

- **Forest Health Scorecard** – Grades forests (A–F) with circular progress visualization.
- **Real-Time Scoring** – NDVI, tree density, rainfall, soil moisture, and temperature automatically feed the score.
- **AI-Powered Insights** – AI analyzes trends, predicts risks, and recommends restoration actions.
- **Trend Analysis & Comparison** – Six-month charts, rankings, and restoration comparisons across all forests.
- **Interactive Map** – Color-coded forest markers visualize ecosystem health at a glance.
- **Reports & Alerts** – Generate PDFs, receive SMS alerts, and track restoration progress.
- **Professional UI/UX** – Nature-inspired design with green/earth tones, smooth transitions, semantic color tokens, and well-organized tabs.
